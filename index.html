**Networking** refers to the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share resources, such as data, files, and internet access. It involves the design, setup, and management of systems that allow devices to communicate with each other over local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), or the internet. Networking ensures efficient, secure, and reliable data transmission between devices, enabling collaboration, communication, and access to information..
